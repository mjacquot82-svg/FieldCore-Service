# FieldCore V1 Roadmap

FieldCore V1 should stay focused on the core service-business workflow instead of adding job chat or message action menus too early.

## Product direction

Do not prioritize job chat or a message action menu for V1 unless a clear customer need appears later.

The primary workflow spine is:

1. Customer management
2. Service locations / properties
3. Recurring services
4. One-off jobs
5. Today’s Route
6. Visit completion
7. Ready to Bill
8. Invoices
9. Payment status / AR tracking
10. Customer history / timeline
11. Export/download business records
12. Demo/test tenant

## Why chat is not a V1 priority

A job chat system would add complexity before the core workflow is stable. For this product, a customer or job timeline is more useful than chat because it creates a clear operational history without forcing every event into a conversation model.

## Timeline-first approach

The preferred replacement for job chat is a timeline/history model that can show:

- service added
- service changed
- one-off job scheduled
- visit scheduled
- visit completed
- visit skipped
- invoice created
- invoice marked paid or partial
- payment received
- attachment added later
- checklist item completed later

## Current V1 priorities

1. Customer service management
2. One-off job scheduling
3. Customer/property timeline
4. Ready-to-Bill cleanup
5. AR dashboard / aging
6. Export/download business records
7. Demo/test tenant

## Later, only if needed

Potential future communication features:

- internal office notes
- crew notes
- customer-facing messages
- job-specific comments
- photo/comment history

These should be built only after the core service, billing, and AR workflow is reliable.
